❓ Game Guide

🗺️ Map Nodes

Explore the interconnected paths of the map to advance floor by floor until you reach the final boss of each world.

  • Normal Battles 🗡️: Grant a level up on victory and heal 15% of your max HP.
  • Keyblade Chests 📦: Unlock stronger weapons or grant valuable accessories.
  • Save Points 💾: Automatically save your journey and restore 40% HP and 50% MP.
  • Moogle Shops ⭐: Choose one accessory for free out of two displayed cards.
  • Mysteries 📜: Random narrative events containing rewards, penalties, or healing.

⚔️ Combat & Spells

Combat resolves automatically in turns where you manage your accumulated MP.

  • MP Regeneration: Recover +5 MP on your turn and another +5 MP when hit.
  • Cure Spell 💚: Cast with a 30% chance if HP is under 40%. Consumes 50 MP and heals based on your Magic.
  • Offensive Magic ✨: Cast with a 30% chance if you have at least 15 MP. Consumes 15 MP and deals magic damage.
  • Double Strike 🗡️🗡️: Chance to strike twice in a row, scaling with your Speed.
  • Dodge Roll 💨: Chance to completely evade incoming attacks, scaling with your Speed.

📈 Character Growth

Enhance your stats by leveling up after each victorious combat node:

  • Max HP (+18): Increases your health pool to survive heavy hits.
  • Strength / ATK (+8): Boosts physical attack damage.
  • Magic / MGK (+3): Scales offensive spell damage and Cure healing power.
  • Max MP (+4): Increases maximum mana capacity.
  • Speed / SPD (+1): Increases dodge and double strike probabilities.

♻️ Inventory & Recycling

You can equip up to 2 accessories simultaneously. Duplicate accessories can be equipped together to stack their stats.

Item Recycling: Hover over any accessory in your inventory and click the ♻️ icon to dismantle it for a permanent random stat bonus:

  • ❤️ +15 Max HP
  • 💙 +5 Max MP
  • ⚔️ +1 Strength (ATK)
  • ✨ +1 Magic (MGK)
